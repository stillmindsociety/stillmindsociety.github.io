Our Communication Philosophy

We practice what we preach about intentional communication. Here's what you can expect when you reach out to Still Mind Society:

Thoughtful, Not Immediate

We don't promise instant responses because we don't believe in instant culture. We take time to read your message carefully and respond meaningfully.

Quality Over Quantity

We'd rather have one meaningful conversation than a dozen superficial exchanges. We're selective about commitments to ensure we can give our full attention to each collaboration.

Human-Centered

No chatbots, auto-responders, or form letters. Every message is read and responded to by an actual human being who cares about your ideas.

Response Timeline

To set proper expectations and respect everyone's time:

  • • General inquiries: 24-48 hours
  • • Partnership proposals: 3-5 business days
  • • Research collaborations: 1-2 weeks
  • • Speaking requests: 1 week

Why the wait? We believe your message deserves thoughtful consideration, not a rushed response. We also practice digital wellness by checking email at scheduled times rather than constantly.

If your inquiry is truly urgent, please indicate that in your subject line and we'll prioritize accordingly.
