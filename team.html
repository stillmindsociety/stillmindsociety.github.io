The Collective

Each of us brings a unique perspective to the challenge of creating balance in our hyperconnected world.

H

Hannah

Interior Designer & Spatial Philosopher

Hannah creates physical spaces that invite pause and reflection. Her work challenges the assumption that every space must be "activated" or "optimized," instead exploring how emptiness and negative space can be generative. She believes our environments shape our mental states more than we realize.

S

Sanaa

Graphic Designer & Visual Minimalist

Sanaa's design philosophy centers on what she calls "visual breathing room." Her work strips away the unnecessary to reveal what's essential, creating designs that feel calm rather than chaotic. She advocates for design that serves the message, not the ego.

M

Matthew

Game Developer & Interactive Storyteller

Matthew creates games that deliberately subvert addictive design patterns. Instead of endless engagement, his games have natural endpoints. Instead of constant rewards, they explore contemplation. He's interested in how interactive media can teach patience rather than demand attention.

K

Kiaryn

Digital Designer & Technology Humanist

Kiaryn specializes in designing digital experiences that respect human attention and energy. Her work focuses on creating interfaces that help people accomplish their goals and then gracefully step away. She's passionate about technology that amplifies rather than replaces human connection.

We're not anti-technology or anti-progress. We're pro-intention. We believe the future belongs to those who can toggle between deep engagement and restorative rest, who can be present in the digital world without losing themselves to it.
