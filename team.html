Meet Our Team

Four creators united by a shared belief that our best ideas emerge not from constant stimulation, but from meaningful pauses and contemplative space.

Creators, Not Consumers

We are Hannah, Sanaa, Matthew, and Kiaryn—an interior designer, graphic designer, game developer, and digital designer who came together around a simple realization: our most breakthrough moments happened during breaks, not during binges.

Our Adventure Time-style avatars aren't just playful—they represent our commitment to approaching serious problems with creativity and wonder. In a world that often takes itself too seriously, we believe humor and humanity are essential tools for change.

Our Collective Journey

We met through various creative communities, each struggling with the same paradox: how to stay creatively vital in an attention economy designed to drain creativity.

The breakthrough came when we realized we weren't fighting technology—we were fighting for intentionality. The enemy isn't our devices; it's the assumption that productivity requires constant input.

Still Mind Society is our answer: a creative collective dedicated to designing experiences that honor both focus and rest, engagement and reflection.

Our Collaborative Approach

How four different design disciplines come together to challenge the attention economy.

Interdisciplinary Thinking

When an interior designer, graphic designer, game developer, and digital designer collaborate, magic happens. We bring different perspectives to every challenge:

  • • Physical space informs digital interface
  • • Visual hierarchy influences game mechanics
  • • Interactive design shapes environmental planning
  • • User experience guides brand storytelling

Attention vs. Boredom

Our avatar style reflects a playful take on a serious battle: attention technology versus boredom as a creative gateway. We've chosen our side—we're team boredom.

Not because we're against technology, but because we believe the most interesting innovations come from the spaces between interactions, the pauses between posts, the silence between sounds.

Working With Still Mind Society

We partner with organizations, brands, and individuals who want to challenge the culture of constant stimulation. Our projects range from digital products to physical spaces, always with the goal of creating more intentional, humane experiences.

What We Offer:

  • • Design consultation on attention-respectful products
  • • Research and strategy for ethical engagement
  • • Workshop facilitation on digital wellness
  • • Creative direction for mindful brand experiences
  • • Space design that supports contemplative work

Our Process

Every project begins with a simple question: "How might we create more space for human flourishing?"

We start by understanding the true goals—not just the stated objectives, but the deeper human needs. Then we design solutions that honor both business requirements and human wellbeing.

Our deliverables always include strategies for sustainable engagement, natural stopping points, and ways to measure success beyond just attention captured.
